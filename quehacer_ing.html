What can I do?

1

Learn

about the species and ecosystems of Mexico, what do humans do to damage them and how can we take care of them.

2

Participate

with organized groups that help conserve nature.

3

Consume and produce responsibly

choose the options that use less energy, less water, and that produce less garbage.

4

Report illegal activities

and ask authorities to protect our environment.
